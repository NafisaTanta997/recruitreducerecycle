Recruit. Recycle. Reward!

A startup project developed by Noshin Azimi, Britany Balkissoon, Nafisa Tanta, and Monique Maile. Our main focus is to minimise the direct impact of plastic that pollutes the environment. According to our research, we found the top reasons for the recurring impacts of pollution on the environment is plastic bottles. Our app builds a platform that will help educate people and make recycling bottles more efficient.
